Delicious Maggi Recipe

Divya Special Quick and Easy Snack

Maggi is a popular instant noodle dish that is quick and easy to make. Follow this recipe to prepare a delicious bowl of Maggi in just a few minutes!

Steps to Prepare Maggi:

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles and tastemaker masala.
  3. Stir well and cook for 2-3 minutes until the noodles are soft.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!

Optional Add-Ons:

  • Chopped vegetables (carrots, peas, capsicum).
  • Grated cheese for extra creaminess.
  • A pinch of chili flakes & oregano for spice.

Ingredients:

Ingredient Quantity
Maggi Noodles 1 Pack
Water 1.5 Cups
Vegetables (optional) 1/4 Cup
Cheese (optional) 1 Tbsp
